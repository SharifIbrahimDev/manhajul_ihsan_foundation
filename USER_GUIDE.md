# 🎯 Quick Start Guide - New Features

## Welcome to Your Improved App! 🎉

This guide will help you discover and use all the new features we've added to make your experience better.

---

## 🔐 **Feature 1: Forgot Password**

### When to Use
- You can't remember your password
- You want to reset your password for security

### How to Access
1. Open the app
2. On the login screen, look for **"Forgot Password?"** link below the login button
3. Click it

### What to Do
1. Enter your email address
2. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
3. Check your email inbox
4. Click the link in the email
5. Create a new password
6. Return to the app and login

### Tips
✅ Make sure to check your spam folder  
✅ The link expires after a few hours  
✅ You can request a new link if needed

---

## 👤 **Feature 2: Edit Your Profile**

### When to Use
- Update your phone number
- Change your address
- Update your profile picture
- Modify your full name

### How to Access
**Method 1: From Dashboard**
1. Login to the app
2. Go to bottom navigation
3. Tap **"Notifications"** or **"Messages"** then **"Dashboard"**
4. Tap **"Profile"** tab
5. Tap **"Edit Profile"** button

### What You Can Edit
- ✏️ Full Name
- 📱 Phone Number
- 🏠 Address
- 📸 Profile Picture (tap camera icon)

### What You Cannot Edit
- 🔒 Email (read-only for security)
- 🔒 Role (only President can change)

### Tips
✅ All fields are required  
✅ Phone must be at least 10 digits  
✅ Changes are saved immediately

---

## 🔑 **Feature 3: Change Password**

### When to Use
- Creating a stronger password
- Regular security updates
- After sharing your password accidentally

### How to Access
1. Go to **Edit Profile** (see Feature 2)
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **"Change Password"**

### What to Do
1. Enter your **current password**
2. Enter your **new password** (min. 6 characters)
3. **Confirm** the new password
4. Tap **"Change Password"**

### Password Tips
✅ Use at least 6 characters  
✅ Mix letters, numbers, and symbols  
✅ Don't use common words  
✅ Don't reuse old passwords

---

## 🔄 **Feature 4: Pull to Refresh**

### When to Use
- Check for new transactions
- Update your data
- See latest information

###  How to Use
1. Go to **"Transactions"** tab
2. Put your finger at the top of the list
3. **Pull down**
4. Release when you see the refresh icon
5. Wait a moment for data to update

### Tips
✅ Works on any scrollable list  
✅ Updates happen quickly  
✅ No need to close and reopen app

## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **Q: I didn't receive the password reset email**
**A:** Check these:
- Is the email address correct?
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (can take 5-10 min)
- Try requesting again

### **Q: My profile changes aren't saving**
**A:** Make sure:
- All fields are filled
- Phone number is valid (10+ digits)
- You have internet connection
- You clicked "Save Changes"

### **Q: Can I change my email address?**
**A:** No, email addresses cannot be changed for security reasons. Contact your administrator if you need to use a different email.

### **Q: Can I change my role?**
**A:** No, only the President can assign roles. Contact your President if you need a role change.

### **Q: How do I add my profile picture?**
**A:** 
1. Go to Edit Profile
2. Tap the camera icon on your profile circle
3. Choose a photo from your gallery
4. Save changes
